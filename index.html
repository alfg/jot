Jot is a simple, distraction-free, note-taking app with sharing, saving and encryption features.

Jot does not store any information. The message is either encoded or encrypted (if using a key) within the URL hash and is decoded upon loading the shared URL or decrypted upon providing the key.

Jot is open-sourced under the MIT License. View, fork, or contribute to the project on Github.
